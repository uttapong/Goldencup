INTRODUCTION
Established in 1950, Golden Cup Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd. is among Thailand’s leading pharmaceutical manufacturers. For over 60 years, Golden Cup Pharmaceutical has served Thailand not only with a very well recognized “Golden Cup Balm”, but also with a number of modern pharmaceutical products under “Golden Cup” trademark.
